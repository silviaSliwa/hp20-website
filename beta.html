About HP2.0

Built for focused Högskoleprovet preparation.

HP2.0 is an iOS app for Swedish Högskoleprovet preparation, combining adaptive practice with structured review and progress insight.

Adaptive learning

Practice adapts to your strengths, weaknesses, and pace.

Historical questions

Train with Högskoleprovet-style material and realistic flows.

Spaced repetition

Review important mistakes before they disappear from memory.

Mistake Bank

Collect confusing questions and close knowledge gaps over time.

Dream Education

Connect study progress to the program you want to reach.

Statistics

Track performance, trends, and category-level progress.

Today Mission

Keep daily practice clear, focused, and easy to resume.

What We’d Like You to Test

Getting started

  • Onboarding
  • Navigation
  • Today Mission
  • Dream Education

Question experience

  • Question flow
  • DTK figures and charts

  • Statistics
  • Mistake Bank

Purchase and quality

  • Premium/paywall clarity

  • Crashes

Report anything that feels unclear, slow, unstable, or visually unfinished.

FAQ

Questions before you join

Who can join the beta?

Anyone with an iPhone who wants to help test HP2.0. The app is primarily useful for Swedish-speaking users preparing for Högskoleprovet.

Is the beta free?

Yes, the TestFlight beta is free to install and test.

Do I need to understand Swedish?

Yes. The app content is in Swedish because it is designed for Högskoleprovet.

How do I leave feedback?

Use the feedback email or the Report a Bug button on this page.

Will my progress carry over?

Progress may reset during beta as the app is still being prepared for launch.

Can I leave the beta later?

Yes. You can stop testing through TestFlight at any time.

When will the app launch?

The goal is to prepare HP2.0 for App Store launch after TestFlight feedback and final QA.
